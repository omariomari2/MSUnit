Welcome to MSUnit

MSUnit is a mobile stroke unit and remote neurologist consultation system to improve patient outcomes and reduce treatment times. The system will enable mobile stroke technicians to collect and transmit patient data to remote neurologists for consultation and guidance.

Select your professional role to access our Mobile Stroke Unit system

Technician

Technicians are crucial first responders who:

  • • Collect initial patient data
  • • Record vital signs
  • • Perform NIHSS assessments
  • • Input patient medical history
  • • Prepare comprehensive patient records

Register as Technician

Neurologist

Neurologists provide expert medical insights by:

  • • Reviewing detailed patient records
  • • Analyzing NIHSS scores
  • • Providing remote consultations
  • • Developing treatment strategies
  • • Making critical diagnostic decisions
